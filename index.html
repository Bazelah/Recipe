Quick and Easy Brownies

Recipe by Chef Bajjo

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white suger
  • 1 cups butter
  • 2 cups white suger
  • 1/2 cups cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups white suger
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions

  1. combine all wet ingredient
  2. combine all dry ingredient
  3. mix all dry and wet ingredients together
  4. pour into pre lined molds
  5. bake 350 degree for 25 mins
  6. let it cool and enjoy!
